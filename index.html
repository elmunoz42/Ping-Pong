Ping-Pong Counting Game

This website will teach you how to play a drinking (or hot pepper eating) game. To play the game, alternate counting up from 1. Each time you count a number that is multiple of 3 say "Ping!" instead of the respective number. Keep counting. If your number is a multiple of 5 say "Pong!". If your number is both multiple of 3 and 5 (like 15) say "Ping-Pong!". If you make a mistake eat a hot pepper or imbibe. Start from the beginning. Proceed with caution and take public transit to get home! The counter bellow will show you an example of the count. Have fun!
